This is a sample chapter. In a real application, chapter content would be loaded dynamically based on the chapter number. The rain had stopped sometime before dawn, but the streets still glistened as if the city itself refused to let go of the night. He stood at the corner of the street, coat damp, fingers numb, staring at the building across from him. Memories flooded back—some welcome, most not. Each window told a story, each shadow held a secret he'd tried to forget. "Sometimes," he whispered to no one, "the quiet is louder than the noise." A light flickered on in a third-floor window. His breath caught. After all these years, could it be? The possibility, however slim, was enough to keep him rooted to the spot, waiting, watching, hoping against hope. End of Chapter 1
