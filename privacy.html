Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At DeadLeaf, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. We've built our platform to respect your data and give you complete control. Reading should be private, peaceful, and personal.

What We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to improve your reading experience:

  • Reading progress — saved locally and synced to your account
  • Theme preferences — stored in your browser
  • Bookmarks — stored in your account for cross-device sync
  • Email address — only if you create an account

Note: We never sell your data. Ever.

Account Data

When you create an account, we store your email and encrypted password. Your reading progress and bookmarks are associated with your account so you can access them from any device.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies.

Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database storage. They process data according to their privacy policy. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any other tracking services.

Your Rights

You have complete control:

  • Delete your account anytime
  • Export your data (coming soon)
  • Choose your theme — no dark patterns

Contact

If you have questions about your privacy, email me directly: max.works.help@gmail.com
